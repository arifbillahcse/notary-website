What Is Remote Online Notarization?

Remote Online Notarization (RON) is the act of performing a notarization remotely using two-way audio-video technology. It is the online equivalent of an in-person, paper-based notarization where every part of the process is done via the internet.

There must be an audio-visual provider or platform between the signer and the remote online notary.

A remote notary is a legally commissioned notary public who is authorized to conduct notarizations over the internet via digital tools and a live audio-video call. All notaries, no matter how they perform their service, must watch as someone signs a document if it has to be notarized.

Approved in Indiana & Nationwide

Nearly all states in the United States have approved Remote Online Notarization (RON), including Indiana State — of which we are a certified Remote Online Notary.
